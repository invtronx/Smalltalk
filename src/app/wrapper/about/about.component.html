Smalltalk

Facts

  • Smalltalk is inspired by Facebook and Twitter, and as such, its structure resembles them greatly.
  • Initially, the developer chose the name "Textbook - Facebook without faces", but that would cause a contradiction because in Smalltalk, there are faces indeed.
  • Smalltalk took nearly a month to build from scratch.
  • Smalltalk is built with the MEAN stack - MongoDB, ExpressJS, Angular and NodeJS.

Bugs

As of August 9, 2020, there have been no bugs but if you encounter any strange behavior, feel free to report them.
